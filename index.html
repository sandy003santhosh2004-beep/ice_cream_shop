Our Story

From the Farm to Your Table

Welcome to Dairy Shop — Kovilpatti's most trusted name for fresh, pure dairy products. For over a decade, we have been sourcing the finest dairy produce directly from local farms and delivering it to your doorstep with care, hygiene, and love.

We believe that great dairy starts with happy, healthy cows and ethical farming practices. Every product you receive carries our promise of freshness, purity, and nutritional goodness.
